Interdisciplinary Coders at West Valley

Coding is usually seen as something Computer Science majors do, but that's not always the case! Coding can be used to do so many things like drawing, creating video games, data analysis, making websites, automating tedious actions, and so so much more.

The Interdisciplinary Coders at West Valley is here to teach you all the ways that coding can be used -- which both can be used within your career or even for personal development.

What we do

Workshops

Every week we have an in-person workshop about a specific way coding can be used.
Then, we'll have an online workshop diving more into that particular lesson!

Projects

Each week will provide an opportunity to apply the skills within the lessons taught --
allowing you to create personal projects.

Community

We bring a community of coders from all fields of study, and we encourage each other to the best of our abilities! Beginners and any majors are welcome!
